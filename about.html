About Us

Who We Are

Pathable is a registered physiotherapy provider founded with a vision to walk alongside people living with disability and older adults in the community.

Our name combines “Path” and “Able” – reflecting our belief that every individual has the right to create their own path in life, with independence, dignity, and well-being.

Meet Our Founder

Hi, I’m Seung Lee – a registered physiotherapist and the founder of Pathable.

With years of experience delivering physiotherapy services in community, aged care, and private practice settings, I founded Pathable to support both NDIS participants and older adults in achieving greater independence, mobility, and quality of life.

My approach is person-centered, culturally aware, and evidence-based – built on the belief that care should be accessible, meaningful, and empowering.

Our Philosophy

At Pathable, we believe that ‘Able’ lives in every ‘Disable’. Our role is to help uncover and strengthen that ability – whether through disability support or aged care services. Every journey is unique, and our care goes beyond treating symptoms – it’s about understanding your story, walking beside you, and empowering you to live with confidence and comfort.

Why Choose Us

We understand that every person’s journey is unique. Our physiotherapy services are tailored to individual goals, abilities, and lifestyle.

We provide home and facility visits, bringing professional support directly to where you feel most comfortable.

We offer physiotherapy-led pain management programs to support aged care facilities. Our services replace the gap left by ACFI’s removal of pain management, ensuring residents continue to receive high-quality care.

As a registered NDIS provider, we prepare all required reports and documentation with full compliance. You can trust that our services meet NDIS quality and safety standards.

We respect cultural diversity and personal preferences, offering flexible scheduling and communication styles to ensure you feel comfortable and supported every step of the way.
