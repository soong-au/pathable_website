About Us

Our Philosophy

At Pathable, we believe that everyone's path to recovery is unique. We are dedicated to providing personalized physiotherapy care that empowers you to move with confidence, overcome challenges, and achieve your goals. Our approach is not just about treating symptoms but understanding your story and supporting you every step of the way.

What We Do

Our experienced physiotherapists offer a comprehensive range of services, including:

  • Functional mobility assessments
  • Strength, balance, and falls prevention programs
  • Post-surgical and neurological rehabilitation
  • Manual therapy and exercise prescription
  • Assistive technology and equipment prescription
  • Home visits for your convenience

Our Vision

We envision a future where high-quality physiotherapy is accessible to everyone. Our commitment extends beyond the clinic, offering flexible home and community visits to ensure you receive the care you need, wherever you are on your journey.
