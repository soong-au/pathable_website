Who We Are

Pathable is a registered NDIS provider founded by a passionate physiotherapist. We support participants in their journey toward independence, dignity, and well-being.

Our Mission

Possible begins here — because at Pathable, we walk the path to independence together.

We believe everyone has the ability to forge their own path, and that ability lives in everyone, regardless of disability. Our role is to support and empower participants to live with dignity, choice, and independence.

Services

  • Physiotherapy (Therapeutic Supports – 0128)
  • Functional mobility assessments
  • Strength, balance and falls prevention
  • Manual therapy and exercise programs
  • Post-surgical and neurological rehab
  • Equipment prescription & reports for assistive tech
  • Home visits available

(Future: expansion into Support Work, Nursing, Allied Health)
