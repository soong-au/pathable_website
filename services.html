Our Services

What We Offer

Our physiotherapy services include:

  • NDIS Registered Supports (Physiotherapy – 0128)
  • Aged Care Physiotherapy & Pain Management
  • Home visits – care delivered directly to your home or facility
  • Strength, balance & falls prevention programs
  • Manual therapy & tailored exercise programs
  • Self-management education to empower daily life

Aged Care Expertise

We provide dedicated pain management programs for aged care residents under the Chronic Condition Management (CCM) program – helping improve comfort, reduce risk of falls, and enhance overall quality of life.

With the recent removal of pain management from the ACFI framework, facilities are now required to take responsibility for this important area of resident care. Pathable is here to bridge that gap by providing consistent, high-quality physiotherapy support that ensures residents continue to receive the pain management they need.
